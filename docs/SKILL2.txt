ID - Spell ID
MP - Mp/Sp use
SP - Casting Speed
ATKIP - Attack IP(IP damage?)
PW - Power
TG - Target
	51 = DMG ON E
	52 = AOE DMG ON E
	53 = DMG ON ALL E
	58 = BUFF ON SELF
	59 = DMG ON E
	5A = AOE DMG ON E
	5B = DMG ON ALL E
	60 = ALL EX CASTER
	61 = BUFF ON A
	62 = AOE BUFF ON A
	6B = HEAL ON ALL A
	6D = BUFF ON ONE A
	E1 = HEAL ON A
	E3 = HEAL ON ALL A
RD - Radius
XP - Experience
V1 - in the debug menu labeled as ext_com, unclear what it does
EL - Element
	10 - Fire
	20 - Water
	40 - Wind
	50 - Thunder
	60 - Ice
	80 - Earth
	90 - Explosion
	A0 - Forest
WP - Weapon
	1 - Knife
	2 - Sword
	4 - Mace
	8 - Axe
	10 - Whip
	20 - Throw
WK - Walk
	0x32(50) = Move
%C - % chance to critical
ET - Effect
	0 - Heal
	3 - Damage
	5 - Status
	7 - Parameters
	9 - Special
EK - Effect kind?
AC - Animation of move
OT - OUT_TYPE?
IC - Icon ID, unused
